addons - - Goddess - 1.36 mb
Here's my Harry world, Harry Resurrects the Goddess. I've spent hundreds of hours on it, trying to get it to the same high level as Episode 1. Like Ben Spees' original game, mine has a story that runs through it, and I've tied some of the game's effects to the thematic threads of the story. For example, the story is about the process of self-transformation, and level 17, which is titled "The Dark Night of the Soul," uses all-black background and foreground tiles, so Harry appears to be traveling through a black world haunted by sprites. The game also includes a puzzle, and to pass through the first room of the last level (level 18, "Crossing the Abyss"), the player must negotiate a maze. It could be solved through an arduous process of trial-and-error, but players who figure out the puzzle will be able to sail through it. I also made extensive use of the "bottomless pit" feature, and used the hidden sprite feature to create the game's hardest level, level 16 (aptly named "Double Trouble"). I hope you like the game. I had a ton of fun creating it.
