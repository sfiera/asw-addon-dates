addons - - Cytheramenupatch - 24 kb
This small patch by Tycho Maudd enables 2 menus in Cythera, an Audio menu and a perfernces menu. The function of the Audio menu is obvious, but the preferences menu has one sepcial fucntion, it can change the frame rate. Changing the frame rate can speed up or slow down the game and can make movement/animations look better or worse. Higher FPS makes better animation. Be sure that you have Cythera version 1.0.4 before trying this patch. It is also recommended that you apply this patch to a COPY of Cythera, rather than the original, as this patch could be quite difficult to reverse.
